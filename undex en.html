Welcome

A modern office can not do without a computer and without IT specialists-in the vernacular of IT. What kind of specialists are they? They are divided into IT specialists who deal with computer equipment and those who deal with computer software. The first ones are a system administrator and a computer hardware development engineer. The second group includes: programmer, site developers. The site developer knows the mechanisms of the Internet. Among those who develop a website-there is a web designer, a web programmer. If the site has already been created, then it is handled by the head of the Internet project or the manager of the Internet project. Its task is to manage the entire site management system,the site administrator, the site moderator, the site content editor. An online store is also an online project. But there are huge Internet portals that are dedicated to information of one kind or another. Each media company has its own website and a team of employees — web programmers, web designers, site administrator and content manager. Separately, we note SEO specialists-a specialist in site optimization and site promotion, there are site analysts, site auditors. Software, software, is developed for business processes of companies of various profiles: for communication companies, for transport companies, for manufacturing companies. The developed programs are checked by the program tester. Companies use ERP systems to manage their document flow. In this regard, the SAP programmer is in demand. Finally, specialists in information security tools are extremely popular in the IT market. Every bank, every company, and every state-owned enterprise needs information protection. Development of information security methods is superintelligent and super-intensive work. If you acquire such an IT profession — you will be provided with a job and money for life and will be able to live in any country.
